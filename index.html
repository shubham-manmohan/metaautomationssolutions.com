About Us

Meta Automation Solutions is a B2B and B2C professional distributor & dealer of industrial electrical products and with purpose of Import, Export and trading of electrical goods in India and other countries. With time and opportunities provided we have emerged from being an electrical goods supplier to providing industrial automation solutions to our customers, which includes Panel Builders, OEMs, End Users, System Integretors and etc.

Industrial Automation Products- Drives(VFD), Soft Starter, PLC, HMI, Servo Motor & Drives, Smart Relays, Scada, Sensors, Terminal block, Ethernet Switch, RJ 45 Connectors, Motor Starters, Limit Switch, SMPS, Power Supply, Glass Relay, Miniature Relay, Push Buttons, Selector Switch, Indicators, Tower Lights, Buzzers, Emergency Switch, Biometric Switch, Beacon, Sirens, Control Stations/Empty enclosure for harmony, Electronic timers, motors, DBR, Main Emergency Switch, Transformers, Pressure Transmitters, Slim Relays, Twilight Switch, etc.

Frequently Asked Questions

Discover Insights into Our Electrical Automation Expertise

  • What types of electrical automation services do you offer?

    We offer a wide range of electrical automation services tailored to meet your specific needs. Our services include but are not limited to PLC programming, industrial control panel design and fabrication, motion control systems, robotics integration, and custom automation solutions.

  • How can electrical automation benefit my business?

    Electrical automation can significantly enhance efficiency, productivity, and safety within your operations. By automating repetitive tasks and processes, you can reduce labor costs, minimize errors, improve product quality, and increase throughput. Additionally, automation enables real-time monitoring and control, empowering you to make informed decisions for optimized performance.

  • Do you provide maintenance and support for installed automation systems?

    Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance and support services to ensure the smooth operation of your automation systems. Our team of experienced technicians can perform routine maintenance, troubleshooting, and repairs to minimize downtime and maximize uptime. Additionally, we provide software updates, system upgrades, and technical support to address any issues promptly.

  • Can you integrate electrical automation solutions with existing equipment?

    Absolutely, we specialize in seamless integration of electrical automation solutions with your existing equipment and infrastructure. Whether you have legacy machinery or modern systems, our team has the expertise to design and implement tailored automation solutions that complement your current setup. Integration not only enhances the functionality of your equipment but also extends its lifespan and improves overall system performance.

  • What industries do you serve with your electrical automation solutions?

    We serve a diverse range of industries including manufacturing, automotive, pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, packaging, logistics, and more. Our versatile expertise in electrical automation allows us to address the unique challenges and requirements of various sectors, helping businesses across different industries streamline their processes, boost productivity, and stay competitive in today's market.
